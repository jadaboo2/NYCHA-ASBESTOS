NYCHA BUILDINGS WITH PENDING ASBESTOS PROJECTS OVER THE PAST TEN YEARS

Over the past ten years to present, there have been some NYCHA buildings that have asbestos removal projects that still remain postponed. This project specifically looks at tar and insulation materials, two of the most asbestos containing materials (ACMs) in New York City that have to be reported to the Department of Environmental Protection (DEP) when minor asbestos projects become major ones.

BY JADA SIMON

How does tar and insulation cause asbestos?

Brooklyn has the most NYCHA residences in the city with asbestos containing materials. 1,536 NYCHA residences in Brooklyn contain ACMs. Tar is often used to coat bricks because it serves as a barrier for moisture thereby maintaing the structure of the projects they are used for. Insulation tends to be used for temperature regulation of pipes to keep them from freezing or losing heat.
